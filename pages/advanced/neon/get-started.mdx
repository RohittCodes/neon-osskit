Sign up for a free account on the [official Neon website](https://console.neon.tech) to get started with Neon. This guide from the Neon documentation will help you get started with Neon, learn the basics of database branching, and understand how to use Neon in your projects.

# Connection guide

Neon offers various ways to connect to your database. But we will majorly focus on Connecting to neon with Prisma, Drizzle, and the serverless driver in this guide. You can learn about all the other ways to connect to Neon in the [official documentation ↗](https://neon.tech/docs/connect/connect-intro).
